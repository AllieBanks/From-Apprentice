01 — About

The story.

"I started my career as a degree apprentice at a global investment bank in 2017. Years after graduating, it is safe to say I was wrong about one thing — I thought I would know everything by the end."

From Apprentice is a YouTube channel and newsletter built around a single idea: learning never stops. It documents a real, ongoing career journey — from degree apprentice to Business Systems Analyst in RegTech — honestly, in real time, while it is still happening.

Not advice from a distance. Not a polished retrospective. The honest, ongoing account of figuring it out in a forever changing industry — at the intersection of regulation and technology, inside one of the most demanding environments in financial services.
